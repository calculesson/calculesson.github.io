How to Play

  1. Pick up your favorite grade level.
  2. Push "START" to start the game.
  3. Write your answer for a question in a box.
  4. Use "notes" if needed.
  5. Push "NEXT" when you have written your answer!
  6. You will get a score when you have answered all prepared questions.
    Aim for a high score!
